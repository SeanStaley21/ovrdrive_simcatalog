OVRDRIVE SIM CATALOG

Every car and track you can drive in our racing simulators. Here's how to find your way around.

Tip: click the OVRDRIVE logo up top any time to come back here.

HOW TO READ THE CATALOG

1
Two tabs: Cars & Tracks
These two tabs switch between the full list of cars and the list of tracks. They look like this:
Cars Tracks
2
Filter with the sidebar
On the left, narrow cars down by brand or category (GT3, Drift, NASCAR, Formula…), or tracks by type and country.
A car card at a glance
Every car shows three quick numbers and a skill tag:
  • HP — horsepower. How much power it makes. Bigger = faster.
  • FT-LB — torque. The "shove" you feel when you hit the gas.
  • RWD / FWD / AWD — which wheels get the power (rear, front, or all four).
  • Colored tag — how hard it is to drive: BEGINNER, INTERMEDIATE, PRO.
Open a car for the full picture
Click any car to see a bigger photo and three rating bars — Grip (how well it sticks in corners), Top Speed, and Braking. More filled bars = better.
A track card
Each track shows a difficulty from 1 (easy) to 5 (extreme), and how many layouts it has — different versions of the same circuit you can race.
